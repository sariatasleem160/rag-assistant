NovaTech Solutions - Employee & Customer Policy Guide
========================================================

ABOUT THE COMPANY
NovaTech Solutions is a software company founded in 2018. We build cloud tools
for small businesses. Our headquarters is in Austin, Texas.

REFUND POLICY
Customers may request a full refund within 30 days of purchase. After 30 days,
refunds are not available. To request a refund, email support@novatech.com with
your order number.

SHIPPING POLICY
Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Free shipping is available on all
orders over $50. Express shipping (2 days) costs $12.99.

CUSTOMER SUPPORT
Support hours are Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM Eastern Time (EST).
We are closed on weekends and U.S. public holidays. Live chat is available on
our website during support hours only.

EMPLOYEE BENEFITS
Full-time employees receive 20 days of paid annual leave per year. Sick leave
is 10 days per year. Health insurance coverage begins on the first day of
employment.

REMOTE WORK POLICY
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. At least 2 days per week
must be spent in the office. Managers must approve the remote schedule in
advance.

IT & SECURITY
Employees must reset passwords every 90 days. To reset a forgotten password,
contact the IT helpdesk at it@novatech.com. IT responds within 24 hours on
business days. Never share your password with anyone.

DATA PRIVACY
NovaTech stores customer data on encrypted servers in the United States. We do
not sell personal data to third parties. Customers may request data deletion by
emailing privacy@novatech.com.
